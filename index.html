Jonathan Roger's DBC Blog

Hello world, my name is Jonathan Roger and I'm a Dev Bootcamp(DBC) student in Columbus Localhost program. Localhost is a pilot program designed to bring DBC to various cities across the country so those who cannot move to San Francisco, Chicago, or New York City can learn to code as well. Prior to DBC the only coding experience I had was some of the beginner tutorials on codeacademy and so I've come a long way since December of 2014! This is our personal blog page that we were asked to keep during DBC. Each week of phase 0, our 9 week online portion, we were asked to contribute both a technical and cultural blog. Although I was often running short on time when I wrote them, I hope you find my insight's interesting. Additionally once I have some projects to show I will include those on the page as well.
